Getting Started

Tap Choose from Photos or Choose from Files, select one or more images, choose your format, quality, and size, then tap Save to Photos, Save to Files, or Share.

Image Converter Plus supports JPEG, PNG, HEIC, WebP, AVIF, and TIFF. You can also combine multiple images into a single PDF. TIFF, AVIF, and PDF require Pro.

Yes. Select multiple images when importing from Photos or Files. With Pro, you can convert an unlimited batch at once. Free users can convert one image at a time.

Converted images go wherever you choose. Save to Photos adds them to your Photo Library. Save to Files lets you pick a folder. Share sends them to another app. You can also view past conversions in the History tab.

Conversion Settings

Tap the Format menu and choose from JPEG, PNG, HEIC, WebP, TIFF, AVIF, or PDF. Keep Original keeps the same format as your source. TIFF, AVIF, and PDF require Pro.

Use the Quality slider or tap a preset (Low, Medium, High, Maximum). Higher quality means larger files. Quality applies to JPEG, HEIC, WebP, and AVIF. PNG and TIFF are always lossless.

Yes. Tap the Size control to choose Small (50%), Original (100%), or Large (200%). Reducing size makes files significantly smaller. Enlarging won't improve image clarity.

With Pro, tap any image thumbnail to set a different format, quality, size, or name for just that image. Other images keep the batch settings.

Privacy & Metadata

Photos contain hidden data like GPS location, date taken, camera model, and device info. Removing this protects your privacy when sharing images online.

With Pro, tap Privacy and enable Location Data. This removes GPS coordinates and place names while keeping everything else.

Location Only removes just GPS data. Personal Metadata removes location, device info, captions, and edit history. All Metadata removes everything except essential appearance data. Custom lets you choose individual categories.

Saving & Sharing

You choose where to save. Save to Photos adds them to your Photo Library. Save to Files lets you pick a folder, including iCloud Drive and connected cloud services. Share opens the iOS share sheet for AirDrop, Messages, Mail, or any other app.

From any app, tap the share button, then choose Convert (you may need to scroll or tap More). The Share Extension uses your default preset. All processing stays on your device.

When enabled in Settings > Default Settings, formats like HEIC, AVIF, and TIFF are automatically converted to JPEG when you share. This ensures your images open on any device, including Android and older systems.

Presets

Presets are saved combinations of format, quality, size, and privacy settings. They let you quickly apply your favorite settings without adjusting each option.

Go to Settings > Presets. Tap Edit to drag presets into your preferred order. You can also hide presets you don't use.

History

Swipe left on any history item and tap Delete. Or tap Edit and select multiple items to delete at once.

Go to Settings > History Options and choose a timeframe: 7 days, 30 days, or 90 days. Older conversions are automatically removed. Choose Never to keep everything.

Pro

Unlimited batch conversions, TIFF, AVIF, and PDF output formats, per-image setting overrides, all privacy and metadata removal options, and all presets including those with privacy controls.

Tap the Pro banner in Settings or tap any Pro-locked feature. Pro is available as a subscription (weekly, monthly, or annual) or as a one-time lifetime purchase. All purchases are handled securely through the App Store.

Go to Settings, scroll to the Pro section, and tap Restore Purchases. This syncs your purchase if you bought Pro on another device or reinstalled the app. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID.
