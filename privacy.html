Privacy Policy

ImageConverter for iOS

Your photos stay yours. ImageConverter processes everything on your device — nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared.

What Stays on Your Device

All your images, conversion history, and preferences are stored only on this device. Nothing ever leaves without your explicit action.

  • Imported and converted images
  • Conversion history and thumbnails
  • Your format and quality preferences

What We Don't Collect

ImageConverter doesn't collect any personal information. We don't know who you are, what you convert, or how you use the app.

  • No personal information
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics
  • No location data
  • No advertising or tracking

Photos & Images

When you select photos, ImageConverter only sees the specific images you choose — never your entire library.

All image processing happens locally using your device's hardware. Converted images are saved to your device and shared only when you choose to share them.

You can strip metadata (EXIF, GPS, camera info) from converted images to protect your privacy when sharing.

Purchases

Purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

Your purchase status is stored locally on your device and verified through Apple's secure systems.

Third-Party Services

ImageConverter uses only Apple's built-in services. There are no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external tracking tools.

The app works completely offline. An internet connection is only needed when making a purchase.

Your Control

You're in control of your data. You can delete your conversion history anytime from the History tab, or remove the app to delete all associated data.

Automatic cleanup is available in Settings to remove old conversions after a period you choose.
