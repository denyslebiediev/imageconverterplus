Privacy Policy

Your photos stay yours. Everything is processed on your device — nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared.

On Your Device

All your images, conversion history, and preferences are stored only on this device. Nothing ever leaves without your explicit action.

  • Imported and converted images in app sandbox storage
  • Conversion history and thumbnails
  • Your format, quality, and resize preferences
  • Temporary files in /var/tmp/ — automatically cleaned on each launch
  • Keychain storage — only a hash of redeemed promo codes, never the code itself
  • App Group container — shares only Pro purchase status with the Share Extension

What We Don't Collect

Image Converter Plus doesn't collect any personal information. We don't know who you are, what you convert, or how you use the app.

  • No personal information
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics
  • No location data
  • No advertising or tracking

Photos & Images

When you select photos, Image Converter Plus only sees the specific images you choose — never your entire library. The app uses limited photo library access and stores only localIdentifiers for selected assets.

If a selected photo is stored in iCloud, the app temporarily downloads it to your device with a 2-minute timeout. These ephemeral copies are cleaned up automatically on launch.

All image processing happens locally using your device's hardware. Converted images are saved to your device and shared only when you choose to share them.

You can remove metadata (EXIF, GPS, camera info, timestamps, author details, device identifiers, edit history, and embedded thumbnails) from converted images to protect your privacy when sharing.

Purchases

Purchases are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit 2. We never see your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details.

Your purchase status is verified locally using Apple's cryptographic transaction verification. No server calls are made to validate purchases.

Promo codes are validated locally using HMAC — only a cryptographic hash is stored in the Keychain, never the code itself.

Share Extension

The Share Extension runs as an isolated process separate from the main app. It converts images using your default preset directly from the system Share Sheet.

Temporary files created by the extension are cleaned up after use. The extension does not persist conversion history. The only data shared between the main app and the extension via App Group is your Pro purchase status.

Data Retention

You're in control of your data. You can delete your conversion history anytime from the History tab. Automatic cleanup is available in Settings — choose 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or keep conversions forever.

On each launch, the app cleans up orphaned files (imports, outputs, and thumbnails not linked to any history record) and removes temporary directories.

Deleting the app removes all associated data — images, history, preferences, and App Group storage. The only exception is a small Keychain entry (promo code hash), which persists across installs as is standard for iOS Keychain items.

Third-Party Services

Image Converter Plus uses only Apple's built-in frameworks. There are no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external tracking tools.

The app works completely offline. An internet connection is only needed when making a purchase.

Privacy Manifest

The app includes an Apple Privacy Manifest declaring NSPrivacyTracking: false. No data types are collected or tracked, and no tracking domains are contacted.
